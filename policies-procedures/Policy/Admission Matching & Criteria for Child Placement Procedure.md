## PURPOSE:

To provide detailed operational guidance for implementing the admission matching process that ensures children are placed with foster families best equipped to meet their specific needs, support their permanency goals, and provide trauma-informed care within the requirements of the T3C system. This procedure streamlines the matching process through digital workflows while maintaining comprehensive assessment standards.

## PROCEDURE:

### 1\. INITIAL REFERRAL RECEIPT

**Purpose and Overview**

The initial referral receipt phase establishes the foundation for successful placement matching. During this critical first contact, staff initiate the digital **Placement Inquiry Workflow** at previewforms.refugehouse.org to capture essential information that will guide all subsequent matching decisions. As stated in our policy, "The matching process utilizes the comprehensive information gathered during screening and assessment to ensure that each placement referral is evaluated to determine the most appropriate match based upon all known factors at the time of referral."

| Who | How | When | Where | Regulatory Reference |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Intake Department | Receive referral and initiate **Placement Inquiry Workflow** through: 1\. Phone call entry 2\. Email integration 3\. Portal sync 4\. Direct input | 24/7 availability | previewforms.refugehouse.org | TAC §749.503 Contract Term 7 |
| Intake Department | System automatically documents: 1\. Date and time stamp 2\. Referring worker information 3\. Urgency level classification 4\. Initial child data capture | Upon workflow initiation | Placement Inquiry Workflow | FC2-01 |
| Intake Department | Workflow generates acknowledgment: 1\. Auto-confirmation to referral source 2\. Expected response timeframe 3\. Request for missing information | Within 15 minutes | Automated via system | Contract Term 7 |

### 2\. INFORMATION GATHERING

**Purpose and Overview**

Comprehensive information gathering ensures placement decisions are based on complete understanding of the child's needs. The **Admission Assessment** form at previewforms.refugehouse.org captures all required elements systematically, eliminating redundant data entry while ensuring compliance with all regulatory requirements. Our policy emphasizes attention to "the child's history, behaviors, needs, personality and strengths, with special attention given to trauma history and its impact on placement needs."

### 3\. FOSTER FAMILY MATCHING

**Purpose and Overview**

The matching process identifies the most appropriate foster family based on comprehensive assessment of both child needs and family capabilities. The digital **Placement Workflow** at previewforms.refugehouse.org automates compatibility scoring while maintaining clinical judgment in final placement decisions. This systematic approach ensures optimal matches while expediting placement for emergency situations.
