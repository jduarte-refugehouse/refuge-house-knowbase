## PURPOSE OF THIS ADDENDUM

This implementation addendum provides specific operational details for IDD/Autism Spectrum Disorder Support Services during the T3C Interim Credential period. While the parent policy (FC-IDD-01) establishes permanent requirements and procedures, this addendum documents:

1. **Current staffing assignments** and responsible parties  
2. **Existing partnerships** and contractual arrangements  
3. **Phased implementation plans** for building full capacity  
4. **Interim solutions** while permanent infrastructure develops  
5. **Specific timelines** for achieving full compliance

**IMPORTANT:** This addendum is a living document updated monthly during the Interim Credential period. As staffing, partnerships, and capabilities evolve, this document will be revised to reflect current operational reality.

## SECTION 3: LICENSED THERAPIST SERVICES

### Current Service Delivery Model

**Provider Network:** Superior HealthPlan/STAR Health Contracted Therapists

**IDD/Autism Requirement:** Therapists must have experience with DSM-5 diagnoses of IDD/Autism

**Current Therapists:**

- STAR Health credentialed  
- IDD/Autism specialization verified  
- Existing relationships maintained where appropriate

**Coordination Process:**

1. Case Managers verify credentials AND IDD/Autism experience  
2. Authorization requests with developmental treatment goals  
3. Therapy frequency with clinical justification  
4. Monthly progress reports integrated into Service Plans

**Gaps and Mitigation:**

- Limited specialized therapist availability in some areas  
- Mitigation strategies include telehealth and expanded provider search
