## PURPOSE OF THIS ADDENDUM

This implementation addendum provides specific operational details for Mental & Behavioral Health Support Services during the T3C Interim Credential period. While the parent policy (FC-MH-01) establishes permanent requirements and procedures, this addendum documents:

1. **Current staffing assignments** and responsible parties  
2. **Existing partnerships** and contractual arrangements  
3. **Phased implementation plans** for building full capacity  
4. **Interim solutions** while permanent infrastructure develops  
5. **Specific timelines** for achieving full compliance

**IMPORTANT:** This addendum is a living document updated monthly during the Interim Credential period. As staffing, partnerships, and capabilities evolve, this document will be revised to reflect current operational reality.

## SECTION 2: LICENSED THERAPIST SERVICES

### Current Service Delivery Model

**Provider Network:** Superior HealthPlan/STAR Health Contracted Therapists

**Current Therapists Serving Refuge House Children:**

- All therapists verified as STAR Health credentialed  
- Existing therapeutic relationships maintained  
- No additional contracting required for Interim period

**Coordination Process:**

1. Case Managers verify STAR Health credentials before referral  
2. Service authorization requests submitted through Superior  
3. Therapy frequency documented with clinical justification  
4. Monthly reports from therapists integrated into Service Plans

**Gaps and Mitigation:**

- No current gaps identified  
- If therapist availability becomes limited, Superior's provider network offers alternatives  
- Telehealth options available for rural or hard-to-serve areas

## SECTION 5: REGISTERED NURSE SERVICES (IDD/AUTISM)

### 24/7 RN Coverage Solution

**Primary Coverage:** Superior HealthPlan STAR Health 24/7 Nurse Advice Line

- **Phone Number:** 1-866-912-6283  
- **Availability:** 24/7/365  
- **Cost:** No additional cost \- included in STAR Health benefits

**Implementation Steps:**

1. **Inactive Period:** Create protocols for appropriate nurse line use  
2. **Staff Training:** All staff trained on when/how to access  
3. **Documentation:** Log all nurse consultations in Radius  
4. **Quality Review:** Monthly analysis of nurse line utilization

**Complex Medical Needs:**

- Request STAR Health Service Coordination for medically complex children  
- Service Coordinator supplements nurse line for care coordination  
- Document as enhanced support in continued stay reviews
