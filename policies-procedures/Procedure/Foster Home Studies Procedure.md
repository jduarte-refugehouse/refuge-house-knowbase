## PURPOSE:

To establish standardized procedures for conducting comprehensive foster home studies that assess family readiness, determine appropriate T3C credentialing levels, and ensure ongoing compliance with regulatory requirements. This procedure integrates digital assessment tools to streamline evaluation while maintaining thorough documentation and quality standards.

## PROCEDURE:

### 1\. INITIAL INQUIRY AND ORIENTATION

**Purpose and Overview**

The initial inquiry phase begins the relationship with prospective foster families. During this phase, we provide information about foster care, assess basic eligibility, and begin building rapport. Our digital systems capture initial information while maintaining the personal connection essential to successful recruitment.

| Who | How | When | Where | Regulatory Reference |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Recruitment Specialist | Process initial inquiries: 1\. Enter contact information in database 2\. Conduct preliminary screening 3\. Schedule orientation session 4\. Send information packet5. Document communication | Within 24 hours of inquiry | Recruitment database | Best practice |
| Recruitment Team | Conduct orientation sessions: 1\. Present foster care overview 2\. Explain T3C service packages 3\. Review basic requirements 4\. Distribute applications5. Answer questions | Monthly or as needed | Office/Virtual platform | TAC §749.855 |
| Administrative Staff | Process applications: 1\. Review for completeness 2\. Create family file 3\. Assign to home developer 4\. Send acknowledgment5. Begin tracking timeline | Within 3 business days | File management system | TAC §749.857 |

### 2\. BACKGROUND CHECKS AND CLEARANCES

**Purpose and Overview**

Comprehensive background checks ensure child safety while respecting applicant privacy. All checks must be completed before proceeding with home visits or interviews. The digital tracking system monitors expiration dates and renewal requirements.

| Who | How | When | Where | Regulatory Reference |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Home Development Specialist | Initiate background checks: 1\. FBI fingerprint-based check 2\. DPS criminal history 3\. Central Registry check 4\. Sex Offender Registry5. Submit all requests simultaneously | Upon application receipt | Background check portal | TAC §749.811-815 |
| Home Development Specialist | Conduct reference checks: 1\. Contact minimum 3 references 2\. Document conversations 3\. Verify employment 4\. Check previous CPA (if applicable)5. Follow up on concerns | Within 14 days | Reference tracking system | TAC §749.1301(a)(2) |
| Supervisor | Review clearance results: 1\. Assess any findings 2\. Determine eligibility 3\. Document decision rationale 4\. Notify family of status5. Plan next steps or denial | Within 48 hours of receipt | Clearance review module | TAC §749.813 |

### 3\. HOME STUDY ASSESSMENT PROCESS

**Purpose and Overview**

The comprehensive home study evaluates all aspects of family functioning, safety, and capacity to provide trauma-informed care. The **Foster Home Credentialing Assessment** at previewforms.refugehouse.org standardizes evaluation while allowing for professional judgment and family uniqueness.

| Who | How | When | Where | Regulatory Reference |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| Home Development Specialist | Complete **Foster Home Credentialing Assessment** including: 1\. Family composition and dynamics 2\. Motivation for fostering 3\. Parenting experience and philosophy 4\. Support system assessment5. Financial stability review | Through home study process | previewforms.refugehouse.org | TAC §749.1301-1335 |
| Home Development Specialist | Conduct individual interviews via assessment tool: 1\. Personal history exploration 2\. Childhood experiences 3\. Previous trauma/loss 4\. Relationship patterns5. Coping mechanisms | Scheduled sessions | Digital interview module | TAC §749.1301(b) |
| Home Development Specialist | Facilitate family interviews using structured format: 1\. Family functioning observation 2\. Communication patterns 3\. Conflict resolution styles 4\. Role definitions5. Family activities | During home visits | Assessment framework | TAC §749.1303 |
| Home Development Specialist | Complete home safety inspection via digital checklist: 1\. Physical environment safety 2\. Sleeping arrangements 3\. Medication storage 4\. Weapon security5. Pool/water safety | During home visits | Safety inspection module | TAC §749.2751-2757 |

**Purpose and Overview**

Based on the comprehensive assessment, the credentialing team determines which T3C service packages the family is qualified to provide. The **Foster Home Credential Verification** system tracks all credentials and training requirements.
