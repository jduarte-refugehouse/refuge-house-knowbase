## PURPOSE

To establish specific procedures for implementing the training requirements outlined in FC-16 Staff and Caregiver Training Policy, ensuring consistent delivery, documentation, and compliance monitoring across all personnel categories and Service Packages.

---

## RESPONSIBILITY

**Training Coordinator:**
- Develop and maintain training schedules
- Coordinate trainer certification and curriculum
- Track compliance and generate reports
- Maintain training documentation systems

**Human Resources:**
- Ensure new staff complete orientation training
- Maintain staff training records
- Address staff training non-compliance

**Home Development Department:**
- Coordinate foster parent pre-service and annual training
- Track caregiver training compliance
- Address caregiver training non-compliance

**Program Director:**
- Oversee training implementation
- Ensure adequate training resources
- Review training effectiveness data

**Treatment Director:**
- Provide clinical input on training content
- Deliver/coordinate clinical training components
- Ensure Treatment Model integration
