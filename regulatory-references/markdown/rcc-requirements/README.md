## Overview

Contract requirements for providers operating under Residential Contracts (RCC) with the Texas Department of Family and Protective Services (DFPS).

---

## Applicability

These requirements apply to:
- Residential treatment centers
- Group homes
- Emergency shelters
- Foster homes with more than 6 children
- Other residential child care operations under RCC contracts
- **Many CPAs also operate under RCC contracts and must comply with both Chapter 749 and RCC requirements**

---

## Relationship to Other Regulatory Documents

### Chapter 749 vs RCC Requirements

- **Chapter 749:** Minimum licensing standards for Child Placing Agencies
- **RCC Requirements:** Contractual obligations for residential care providers
- **Many CPAs operate under both** and must comply with both frameworks
