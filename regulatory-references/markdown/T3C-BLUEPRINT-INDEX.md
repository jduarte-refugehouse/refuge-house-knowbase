## Key Requirements Relevant to Refuge House

### T3C Basic Foster Family Home Support Services (Pages 47-55)

**Core Requirements:**
1. **Foster Family Home Credentialing** - Valid for 4 years, separate credentials for base package and add-ons
2. **CANS 3.0 Assessment** - Annual assessments for youth over age 3
3. **Foster Family Support** - 24/7 support availability, transportation coordination, documentation
4. **Service Denial Response** - Agency covers denied services
5. **Therapy Services** - Regular and frequent individual and family therapy
6. **Aftercare Services** - 6 months following discharge (months 1-3: weekly contact, months 4-6: twice monthly)

**Referenced in:**
- FC-T3C-01 (T3C Basic Foster Family Home Support Services Policy)
- FC3-01 (Individual Service Planning Policy)
- FC14-01 (Discharge and Permanency Planning Policy)
- FC-AF-01 (Aftercare Services Policy and Procedure)

### Mental & Behavioral Health Support Services (Pages 76-86)

**Key Requirements:**
1. **Treatment Director** - Masters-level mental health professional with 3+ years experience
2. **Licensed Therapist Services** - Individual, family, and group therapy
3. **Behavioral Support Specialist/Mentor** - 1:15 ratio, specialized training
4. **Crisis Management Staff** - 1:25 ratio, 24/7 availability
5. **Comprehensive Clinical Assessment** - Enhanced assessment requirements
6. **Continued Stay Reviews** - Every 90 days with written confirmation (p.85)
7. **Mandatory Aftercare** - 6 months, twice-monthly contact minimum (p.86)

**Referenced in:**
- FC-MH-01 (Mental & Behavioral Health Support Services)
- FC2-01 (Admission Assessment Policy)
- FC2-01.1 (Admission Assessment Procedure)
- FC14-01 (Discharge and Permanency Planning Policy)
- FC-AF-01 (Aftercare Services Policy and Procedure)
- FC-04 (Crisis Management Policy and Procedure)

### IDD/Autism Spectrum Disorder Support Services (Pages 123-135)

**Key Requirements:**
1. **Registered Nurse Consultation** - 24/7 availability
2. **Developmental Assessment** - Comprehensive evaluation of functioning
3. **Communication Assessment** - Evaluation of communication needs and methods
4. **Functional Skills Assessment** - Daily living skills evaluation
5. **Environmental & Sensory Assessment** - Environmental modifications and sensory needs
6. **Continued Stay Reviews** - Every 90 days with written confirmation (p.133-134)
7. **Mandatory Aftercare** - 6 months, twice-monthly contact minimum (p.134-135)

**Referenced in:**
- FC-IDD-01 (IDD/Autism Spectrum Disorder Support Services)
- FC2-01 (Admission Assessment Policy)
- FC2-01.1 (Admission Assessment Procedure)
- FC14-01 (Discharge and Permanency Planning Policy)
- FC-AF-01 (Aftercare Services Policy and Procedure)
- FC-04 (Crisis Management Policy and Procedure)
