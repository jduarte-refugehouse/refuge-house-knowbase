## Key Compliance Areas

### Service Delivery (Contract Term 7)

**Requirements:**
- Comprehensive service package implementation
- Case management standards and ratios
- Documentation and reporting requirements
- Quality assurance and continuous improvement
- Aftercare service delivery
- Service coordination and collaboration

**Impact on Operations:**
- All service delivery policies must align with Contract Term 7
- Documentation standards must meet contract requirements
- Quality metrics must be tracked and reported per contract terms
- Aftercare services must meet contract specifications

### Alternative Care (Contract Term 5)

**Requirements:**
- Alternative and substitute care arrangement protocols
- Service coordination for alternative placements
- Documentation requirements

**Impact on Operations:**
- FC18-01.1 (Alternative and Substitute Care Procedure) must comply with Contract Term 5

### Service Coordination (Contract Term 8)

**Requirements:**
- Inter-agency collaboration protocols
- Service coordination standards
- Communication requirements

**Impact on Operations:**
- Service coordination procedures must align with Contract Term 8

### Discharge and Permanency (Contract Term 10)

**Requirements:**
- Discharge planning protocols
- Permanency achievement standards
- Transition support requirements

**Impact on Operations:**
- FC14-01 (Discharge and Permanency Planning Policy) must comply with Contract Term 10
