# SCOPE OF WORK AMENDMENT

## T3C Billing, Cost Reporting, and Claims Administration

**Between:** Refuge House, Inc. and Refuge House San Antonio, Inc. ("Agency")  
**And:** Tracy Corliss / Corliss Financial Services ("Contractor")  
**Effective Date:** \[Date\]  
**Amendment to:** Existing Financial Services Agreement

---

## PURPOSE

This Scope of Work Amendment documents Contractor's fulfillment of the T3C Infrastructure Function for **Billing, Cost Reporting, and Claims Administration** and adds T3C-specific requirements to existing financial services.

**T3C Requirement:** "Identified personnel and infrastructure to support billing, cost reporting, and claims administration"

**Contractor Role:** Tracy Corliss/Corliss Financial Services fulfills this required T3C Infrastructure Function.

---

## EXISTING SERVICES (UNCHANGED)

Contractor continues all existing financial services including:

* Complete financial management and accounting  
* Accounts Payable and Accounts Receivable  
* Payroll processing and expense reimbursements  
* Foster parent/provider reimbursements  
* Tax filing and reconciliation  
* State filing requirements  
* Annual DFPS cost report preparation  
* All other services per existing agreement

---

## T3C-SPECIFIC ENHANCEMENTS (ADDED)

### 1\. Service Package Rate Management

* **Maintain billing rates for each T3C Service Package:**  
    
  * Basic Foster Family Home Support Services  
  * Mental & Behavioral Health Support Services  
  * Intellectual or Developmental Disability (IDD)/Autism Spectrum Disorder Support Services  
  * Add-On Services: Transition Support, Kinship Caregiver Support, Pregnant & Parenting Youth Support


* **Foster parent payment processing by Service Package:**  
    
  * Implement Service Package-specific payment rates  
  * Process pass-through rates based on Service Package assignment  
  * Link payments to placement data provided by Agency from Radius system

### 2\. Service Package Financial Tracking

* **Track revenue and costs by Service Package:**  
  * Separate accounting for each Service Package type  
  * Monitor Service Package profitability  
  * Generate financial reports showing revenue by Service Package  
  * Allocate shared costs appropriately across Service Packages

### 3\. Claims Management by Service Package

* **Code claims by Service Package type:**  
  * Ensure accurate Service Package identification on all claims  
  * Track claim status by Service Package  
  * Monitor Service Package reimbursement rates  
  * Generate Service Package-specific revenue reports

### 4\. T3C Cost Reporting

* **Prepare T3C-specific cost reports:**  
  * Annual cost reports including Service Package breakdowns  
  * Service Package cost allocation documentation  
  * Support DFPS audits of T3C cost data  
  * Maintain audit-ready Service Package financial documentation

### 5\. System Coordination

* **Coordinate with Agency's Radius system:**  
    
  * Receive placement and Service Package data from Radius  
  * Verify placement information with Agency staff as needed  
  * Process payments based on verified Service Package assignments


* **Provide financial data for CQI:**  
    
  * Generate Service Package financial metrics for quality improvement reporting  
  * Support data-driven decision making for Service Package development
